Chain of Command

1. Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

The CEO oversees all operations and strategic decisions.

2. Chief Operations Officer (COO)

The COO manages daily operations and ensures efficiency.

3. Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

The CFO handles financial planning, budgeting, and reporting.

4. Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

The CMO leads marketing strategies and brand development.

5. Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

The CTO oversees the technology infrastructure and innovation.

6. Vice President of Human Resources

The VP of HR manages recruitment, employee relations, and HR policies.

7. Director of Sales

The Director of Sales leads sales teams and business development.

8. Senior Project Manager

The Senior Project Manager coordinates project activities and timelines.

9. Team Lead

The Team Lead supervises a specific team and ensures productivity.
