Flint County Roleplay - Rules

General Rules

  1. Respect all players and staff members.
  2. Do not use offensive language or engage in hate speech.
  3. Follow server guidelines for roleplaying and character behavior.

Roleplay Rules

  1. Remain in-character (IC) at all times.
  2. Do not powergame or metagame.
  3. Obey traffic laws and server-specific rules during roleplay scenarios.

Server-Specific Rules

  1. Use only approved mods and scripts.
  2. Report any bugs or issues to staff immediately.
  3. Do not engage in cheating or exploit server mechanics.
